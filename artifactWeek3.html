Ecosystem Map: Structure of Modern U.S. Universities

Primary User Groups

Students

Learning, credentialing, career preparation

Faculty

Teaching, research, governance

Administration

Operations, finance, compliance

Staff

Support services, advising, IT

Government

Funding, regulation, accreditation

Industry

Research funding, hiring pipelines

System Flow

Federal/State Funding → University Administration → Colleges & Departments → Faculty → Students → Workforce / Society

Core Infrastructure

  • Learning Management Systems (Canvas, Blackboard)
  • Student Information Systems
  • Financial Aid Platforms
  • Research Labs
  • Housing & Campus Services
